Background

Twenty One Pilots were originally created in 2009 by the lead vocalist, Tyler Joseph. Josh Dun then later joined the band in 2011. The band originally was created with three members, but two of them quit in 2011.

Twenty One Pilots

Twenty One Pilots is a band consisting of two people, Tyler Joseph and Josh Dun. The band published two self-released albums before being signed by Fueled by Ramen in 2012. They later acheived massive success with their fourth album, Blurryface. Their single, Heathens also reached success and the band became the first alternative artist to have two concurrent top ten singles in the U.S.
